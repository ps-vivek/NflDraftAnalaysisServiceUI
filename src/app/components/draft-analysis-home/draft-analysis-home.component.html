For starters, drafts are the medium through which nations best players from colleges step into the NFL arena. Also, the team which had the worst record last year gets to pick first in the draft. Likewise, team which won a championship last year, gets to pick last in a draft. In summary, teams pick in the reverse order of the standings that they finished last season.
